Question: How Can I Get My Card Blocked ?

Answer: Please call our 24x7 Call Centre on the numbers given at the top of the screen, and get the card Hot Listed (blocked). For credit cards, you can also call the Credit Card Call Centre on 1800 22 22 44 or 080-25300175 Please also ask for a new card to be issued. In case the old card is found later, please destroy it since it cannot be unblocked.
